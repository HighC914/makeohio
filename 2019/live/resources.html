Innovation Studio

The Innovation Studio is a roving makerspace that is currently set up in Meiling Hall on south campus, but they've brought some of their tools north for you to use!


If you want to use a laser-cutter, 3D printers, or need other tools, visit their set-up on the West side of the center space - near KSA Cafe!




Bosch


Bosch has brought 3D printers and power tools! If you want to use these tools go to Room 177.




Knowlton Fab-Lab

On Saturday the Knowlton Fabrication Lab is open from 12-6pm for Architecture students and they have graciously allowed us to use their space!

ShowOHI/O


We are so proud of everything teams accomplish at our events and we want to show them off to the OSU and Columbus community through ShowOHI/O!


This is a great networking opportunity and a chance to talk more about what you've created. This year's ShowOHI/O is April 5th, if you're interested in bringing a project please reach out to hackohio@osu.edu.


We're also hoping to partner with clubs and companies to put on more entrepreneurial track workshops, to keep up to date with these opportunities, check out our website hack.osu.edu!



Innovation Studio


After MakeOHI/O all teams are eligible for additional funding from the Innovation Studio and are encouraged to pitch their idea to the studio at their next pitch day on February 20th! Click here to submit your MakeOHI/O project to the Innovation Studio.


The Innovation Studio is also opening a permanent space in addition to their movable unit, so they are hiring! To learn more, head down to their set-up in the center space and talk to Josh!
